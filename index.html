Who We Are

Not an Organisation.
A Movement.

We are Rebuilders — a passionate, interdenominational community of young people united for God. We are NOT an organisation; we are a movement led by the Spirit of God.

We exist to rebuild the broken temple of worship and restore it as a daily lifestyle — raising a selfless generation to strengthen His Church.

Every young person who comes to Rebuilders finds a community that helps them investigate, repair, remodel, and build their life firmly on Biblical truth.

Zech 4:9–10

"The hands of Zerubbabel have laid the foundation of this house; his hands shall also finish it."

Our Vision

Raising a Selfless Generation for God to Strengthen His Church.

A generation that worships not out of obligation, but as a way of life — built on the Word, led by the Spirit.

Our Commitment to the Local Church

A vital part of our vision is unity. We are a movement, not a replacement for the local church. Our goal is never to draw young people away from their home congregations or to establish a new denomination. Like scaffolding, we exist to support and build up the Church, that is, by equipping youth to return to their own congregations ready to serve, lead, and strengthen their spiritual homes.

Our Process

How We Rebuild

A hands-on, spiritual approach — walking with each young person through every stage.

01

Inspect & Investigate

We counsel and help young people investigate the root causes behind their spiritual struggles and falls.

02

Make Repairs

We guide individuals in making necessary life repairs — removing what is not from God and replacing it with Godly attributes.

03

Remodel the Architecture

We help young people discover and step into God's absolute best plan and purpose for their lives.

04

Build

We build lives firmly on Biblical doctrines and the leading of the Holy Spirit — brick by brick, truth by truth.

05

Provide Protection

We maintain a lifestyle of praise and worship through regular fellowship, ensuring the newly built walls stay strong.

06

Conquer New Frontiers

We actively identify damaged areas in the world around us so we can conquer and rebuild those places for God.

The Meaning Behind Our Logo

Every Line Has Purpose

The plumbline at the centre represents the rightful alignment required for building a strong, lasting wall.

Within it, the 'Z' carrying the numbers 4910 stands for Zechariah 4:9–10 — our ultimate inspiration and call to action.

The bold 'R' and circular form speak to our identity — a rounded community, centred and aligned, built around His Word.
